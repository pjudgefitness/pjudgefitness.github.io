About Patrick

I've spent years coaching people who didn't think the gym was for them. The ones who walked in skeptical, resistant, or just plain tired of programs that treated them like a number. What I've learned is that the physical and mental sides of training are inseparable — when your body feels strong, your mind follows. And when the process feels right, the results take care of themselves.

I'm based in Charleston, SC. I coach remotely through Trainerize. Every program I write is built from scratch for you — your goals, your schedule, your life.
